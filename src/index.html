Introducing the first Ron Balicki Award, a scholarship bestowed upon an up-and-coming journalist that is dedicated to the craft of telling college golf's most important stories.

Multiple organizations combine efforts to honor the late Hall-of-Famer, a longtime Golfweek writer, columnist and visionary in what college golf means to the overall sport's landscape

Who is Ron Balicki?

"Wrong Ron" covered the game for more than 30 years, from the Masters to elite amateur events to the college game. Balicki is the only media member inducted into the GCAA Hall of Fame. Many credit the growing popularity of college golf to Balicki's passion for covering the game.

What is this award?

Many scholarships are awarded for those that take the course in college, but not many reward those that bring the rounds to life through words, photos and videos. This award will act as a scholarship for a young writer that is dedicated to the journalism that Balicki dedicated himself to for decades.

How can you help?

Part of what Balicki stood for was the entire college golf community, and this is an award that will succeed with the donations from the golf community in general. If you are interested in supporting this scholarship, click here to find more details about how you can help.

Who is involved?

Balicki's impact wasn't only felt at Golfweek, but also across the media landscape. With this award, Golfweek and Global Golf Post are both offering support, while the Golf Coaches Association of America has pledged its support as well.
